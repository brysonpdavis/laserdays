MFPP 1.0.0.1
------------

Thank you for buying Modular First Person Player!

If you are unpacking this for the first time, do not forget to add a "Ladder" layer to the Tags and Layers settings! This will ensure that the ladder module on the default prefabs will work properly on the example scenes.
